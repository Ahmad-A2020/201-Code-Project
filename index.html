Job Experience

from 2018 to 2021, I worked in NGO organization called INTERSOS as WASH TEchnical engineering. I was in charge over the follwing projects:

  • WAAD Project- funded by the European Union (EU-MADAD): The objective of the project is to provide the Jordanian community hosting Syrian refugees with improved WASH infrastructure (through the install of a public pipeline network and erected of water pump station), and facilities at community (rehabilitation and construction work for ten schools in the north of Jordan), and households level (renovation for the sanitary facilities in 400 households).
  • Inclusive educational spaces for Jordanians and Syrians in the governorate of Ajloun- funded by the Italian Agency for Development Cooperation (AICS): In this project, three schools located in the Ajloun governorate were rehabilitated.
  • Improving safe and equitable access to WASH infrastructure- funded by the United Nations Office for the Coordination of Humanitarian Affairs (OCHA): the project aims to renovate the sanitary facility for 480 beneficiaries.
